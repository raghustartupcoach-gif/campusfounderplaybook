About the Author

Nearly three decades. One mission.

Dr. Raghu Pradeep Nair is a Startup Coach and Founder Mentor with nearly three decades of experience spanning corporate law, HR leadership, management education, and institutional governance.

He has trained over 2,000 students across India's finest institutions in Design Thinking, UX Design, Career Planning, and Entrepreneurship — and has watched, up close, what separates the founders who build something lasting from those who give up.

He is the author of The Founder's Edge — a LinkedIn newsletter on startup coaching and campus entrepreneurship — and an active contributor to India's startup ecosystem through mentorship, workshops, and institutional advisory work.
